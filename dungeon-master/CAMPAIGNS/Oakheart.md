# Campaign Log

## Session 1
- **Party:** Kriv (Blue Dragonborn Paladin), Elara (Half-Elf Bard NPC)

### Major Events
- **Arrival:** Kriv arrived in Oakhaven and learned of the Old Keep's curse.
- **Investigation:** Recruited Elara the Bard and Thorne the Gravekeeper.
- **Infiltration:** Entered the Old Keep via the Servant's Path.
- **The Source:** Discovered the Heart of the Keep corrupting the land.
- **Victory:** Destroyed the Heart and defeated the Necromancer Malakar.

### Minor Encounters
- **Tavern:** Won a card game against locals.
- **Wine Cellar:** Found a Potion of Healing.
- **Hallway:** Ambushed by Skeletons.
- **Ossuary:** Ambushed by Plague Zombies.

### Bosses
- **The Wight (Great Hall):**
    - **Status:** Defeated.
    - **Details:** Kriv was knocked unconscious but revived by Elara. Kriv decapitated the Wight.
- **Lady Avelina (The Banshee):**
    - **Status:** Pacified.
    - **Details:** Persuaded to find peace using the Baron's Ring. She opened the path to the Source.
- **Malakar (The Necromancer):**
    - **Status:** Defeated.
    - **Details:** Kriv used Divine Smite to break his defenses. The Heart of the Keep was shattered.

## Session 2
### Major Events
- **Loot Distribution:** Kriv and Elara are organizing the spoils from the Old Keep.
- **Shopping:** Party visited the Oakhaven Market.
- **Thorne:** Visited Thorne at the cemetery. Gave him a cloak (5gp). Confirmed the destruction of the Source.
- **Journal:** Kriv read the Rogue's Journal and found a farewell letter to Widow Miller.
- **Quest:** Heading to Widow Miller's cottage to deliver Kaelen's letter.
- **Widow Miller:** Kriv asked to enter. Widow Miller invited the party inside.
- **Widow Miller:** Delivered Kaelen's letter. Kriv refused the gold reward (returned 10gp) but kept the Silver Locket. Widow Miller asked for help with fences/wolves. Kriv accepted: Elara repairing fences, Kriv patrolling for wolves.
- **Patrol:** Kriv used Divine Sense in the woods (Result: No Undead/Fiends detected).
- **Encounter:** Kriv made a loud noise (Intimidation 16) and attracted 2 Wolves.
- **Combat:** Kriv intimidated the wolves (Frightened), then cleaved a boulder to drive them off. Wolves fled.
- **Reunion:** Kriv returned to the cottage. Elara finished fence repairs. Widow Miller served a meal.
- **Rumors:** Widow Miller mentioned a missing caravan and trouble in the High Pass. Suggested speaking to Garrick at the General Store.
