# Campaign Log: The Capital (Aethelgard)

## Session 1 (Continuation)
- **Party:** Kriv (Blue Dragonborn Paladin - Level 3), Elara (Half-Elf Bard NPC - Level 3)
- **Date:** Day 4 of Travel (Afternoon)

### Major Events
- **The Obsidian Eye:** The party entered Varrick's shop in the Magic District.
- **Browsing:** Varrick displayed his special wares:
    - Potion of Invisibility (200gp)
    - Scroll of Vampiric Touch (150gp)
    - Shadow Shard (50gp)
    - Dagger of Venom (500gp)
- **Appraisal:** Varrick identified the Baron's Signet Ring as a **Ring of Mind Shielding**. Offered 200gp for the Ring and 150gp for the Necromancer's Spellbook (Total: 350gp).
- **Haggle:** Kriv and Elara negotiated hard. Sold Ring, Spellbook, and Original Crypt Map (Kept Copy) for **650gp**. Received a wax mold of the ring.
- **Quest Hooks:** Varrick offered three leads:
    1. **Sewer Bounty:** Hunt monsters in the sewers (Gold + potential loot).
    2. **The Arena:** Gladiatorial combat (Fame + Duke's Favor).
    3. **Haunted Manor:** Exorcise the Vane family home (High Reward).
- **Quest Selection:** Kriv chose the Arena. Asked Varrick to hold the Dagger of Venom.
- **The Grand Coliseum:** Party arrived at the Arena. Registration for "Beast Brawl" is open (50gp buy-in).
- **Arena Intel:** Registrar revealed the opponent is a **Manticore** (CR 3).
- **Intimidation:** Elara and Kriv intimidated the registrar. Entry fee (50gp) waived.
- **Arena Combat:** Kriv intimidated the Manticore (Roll: 21). Manticore is Frightened. Initiative rolled.
- **Combat Round 1:** Elara drank Hill Giant Potion. Kriv used Breath Weapon (11 dmg). Manticore missed all attacks (Disadvantage).
- **Combat Round 2:** Elara cast Faerie Fire. Kriv Dodged. Manticore hit Elara (7 dmg).
- **Combat Round 3:** Kriv cast Command ("Grovel"). Manticore failed save, fell 40ft (15 dmg), and is Prone. Elara hit Manticore (9 dmg).
- **Combat Round 4:** Kriv used Divine Smite (23 dmg). Elara healed herself (+5 HP) and killed the Manticore (9 dmg).
- **Victory:** Kriv harvested Manticore Spikes (6) and Hide. Elara performed for the crowd.
- **Reward:** Collected 200gp prize + 10gp tips.
- **XP Award:** Gained 350 XP each for defeating the Manticore.
- **Purchase:** Returned to Varrick. Bought **Dagger of Venom** for 500gp (Split cost 250gp each). Elara equipped it.
- **Intel:** Asked Varrick about blacksmiths. Learned of **Thalor Iron-Eye** (Artisan's Quarter - High End) and **Gromm** (Lower City - Discreet/Monster Parts).
- **Travel:** Party descended into the Lower City to find Gromm.
- **Crafting:** Commissioned Gromm to craft **Cloak of the Manticore** and **Returning Javelins** (300gp total, split evenly). Consumed Manticore parts.
- **Item Transfer:** Kriv equipped the Cloak of the Manticore and gave the Returning Javelins to Elara.
- **Rest:** Party found a cheap inn, *The Rusty Anchor*, in the Lower City (1gp). Took a Long Rest.
- **Arena Return:** Party returned to the Grand Coliseum. Registrar offered three events: The Gauntlet, The Duel, or The Beast Pack.
- **Duel Registration:** Kriv signed up for "The Duel" vs Titus the Unbroken. Elara signed up for a skirmisher bout (100gp prize).
- **Duel Start:** Kriv cast *Bless* on himself. Titus closed the distance (Dash).
- **Duel Round 1:** Kriv hit Titus with *Ensnaring Strike* (15 dmg), but Titus resisted the restraint. Titus hit Kriv (10 dmg).
- **Duel Round 2:** Kriv cast *Command* ("Throw"). Titus failed save and threw his sword (Disarmed). Titus failed to Shield Bash Kriv.
- **Duel Round 3:** Kriv hit Titus (12 dmg). Titus used *Second Wind* (+11 HP) and hit Kriv with a dagger (6 dmg).
- **Duel Round 4:** Kriv used *Spike Volley* (7 dmg) and hit Titus (10 dmg). Titus hit Kriv with dagger (6 dmg).
- **Duel Round 5:** Kriv drank Potion of Greater Healing (+17 HP). Titus hit Kriv (7 dmg).
- **Duel Round 6:** Kriv retreated (provoking Opportunity Attack - 6 dmg) and used Breath Weapon (4 dmg). Titus charged and hit Kriv (7 dmg). Kriv at 1 HP.
- **Duel Round 7:** Kriv used *Nature's Wrath*. Titus failed save (Restrained), missed attack (Disadvantage), then broke free.
- **Duel Round 8:** Kriv used Lay on Hands (+15 HP). Titus hit Kriv with dagger (6 dmg).
- **Duel Round 9:** Kriv hit Titus (13 dmg). Titus hit Kriv (6 dmg). Both fighters are critical.
- **Duel Round 10:** Kriv struck Titus with the flat of his blade (Non-lethal). Titus fell unconscious. Kriv won.
- **Elara's Bout:** Elara won her skirmisher match.
- **Rewards:** Party collected 250gp total (150 Kriv, 100 Elara). Kriv chose the **Bag of Tricks (Gray)** as his prize.
- **Short Rest:** Kriv spent Hit Dice to heal to full HP.
- **Trade:** Kriv traded the **Bag of Tricks** to Elara for the **Returning Javelins**.
- **Exploration:** Party left the Coliseum to explore the city streets.
- **The Platinum Spire:** Kriv visited the temple of Bahamut to pay respects.
- **Temple Store:** Priest offered Holy Water for 25gp/flask.
- **Purchase:** Kriv bought 4 Flasks of Holy Water (100gp).
- **Roleplay:** Elara played a melancholic song in the temple, enhancing the atmosphere.
- **XP Award:** Gained 500 XP each for the Arena Duels (Titus & Skirmish).
- **The Arcaneum:** Party visited the mages' tower. Researched the "Iron Legion". Learned King Grol has united goblinoid tribes at Cragmaw Castle and is seeking magical artifacts.
- **Recruitment:** Kriv looked for a spellcaster to join the party. Orla directed them to the Battlemage Lounge. Met three candidates: Gimble (Gnome Wizard), Seraphina (Human Sorcerer), Malros (Half-Elf Warlock).
- **Apprentice Found:** Kriv looked for a student to mentor. Found **Felix**, a talented but broke Level 1 Wizard. He offers his services for free in exchange for protection and experience.
- **The Test:** Kriv challenged Felix to cast *Magic Missile* on him. Kriv's **Brooch of Shielding** negated the damage. Felix was impressed and joined the party.
- **Mentorship:** Kriv gave Felix the **Scroll of Fog Cloud** to study.
- **Return to Varrick:** Party returned to The Obsidian Eye to find spells for Felix.
- **Departure:** Kriv decided Felix wasn't ready for high-level spells yet. Party left for the sewers to train Felix.
- **Sewer Encounter:** Party entered the sewers. Encountered a Gelatinous Cube and 4 Giant Rats.
- **Combat Round 1:** Elara summoned a Giant Boar (Bag of Tricks). Felix killed a rat with Fire Bolt. Kriv used Breath Weapon (9 dmg), killing the remaining rats and damaging the Cube. Kriv avoided being Engulfed.
- **Combat Round 2:** Giant Boar charged Cube (16 dmg). Kriv flanked and hit Cube (11 dmg). Elara and Felix hit Cube (8 dmg total). Cube hit Boar (12 acid dmg).
- **Combat Round 3:** Felix cast *Magic Missile* (10 dmg). Boar, Kriv, and Elara hit Cube (26 dmg total). Cube hit Boar (10 acid dmg). Cube is critical (4 HP).
- **Victory:** Kriv finished off the Cube.
- **Loot:** Found 50gp, Peridot (50gp), Potion of Acid Resistance.
- **Level Up:** Party reached **Level 4** (2858 XP).
- **Level 4 Choices:** Kriv took **Heavy Armor Master** feat. Elara took **War Caster** feat.
- **Bounty Claimed:** Party returned to surface. Collected 100gp bounty from City Watch.
- **Shopping:** Returned to Varrick's. Bought Scribing Supplies for Felix (50gp).
- **Opportunity:** Varrick offered access to Malakar's Spellbook (50gp fee per spell).
- **Buyback:** Kriv persuaded Varrick to sell the **Necromancer's Spellbook** back for 150gp (the original offer price) to help Felix.
- **Transaction:** Sold Peridot to Varrick for 50gp.
- **Rest:** Rented rooms at *The Rusty Anchor* (2sp).
- **Mentorship:** Kriv instructed Felix to copy spells into the new book and refurbish it with a patch of Manticore leather.
- **Departure:** Party decided to return to Oakhaven to train Felix and investigate the crypts.
- **Travel:** Joined a caravan of stonemasons heading to Oakhaven.

### Current Status
- **Location:** Traveling.
- **State:** Transit.
- **Active Quest:** None.

## Session 5: The Return
- **Party:** Kriv (Lvl 4), Elara (Lvl 4), Felix (Lvl 4).
- **Companions:** Scraps (Owlbear), Antlers (Summoned Giant Elk).
- **Travel:** Fast pace from Oakhaven.

- **Arrival:** The party has reached the outer gates of Aethelgard.
- **Entry:** Kriv used his reputation ("Slayer of Shadows") to bypass the gate guards.
- **Interaction:** Party visited Varrick at *The Obsidian Eye*.
- **Quest Update:** Varrick renewed the "Haunted Manor" quest (Vane Manor in Noble District). Reward: 600gp + Loot.
- **Appraisal:** Varrick offered 75gp (cash) or 100gp (trade) for the **Scroll of Scorching Ray**.
- **Inquiry:** Kriv asked for other 2nd-level spells.
- **Stock Update:** Varrick offered scrolls of *Flaming Sphere*, *Suggestion*, and *Mirror Image* (150gp each).
- **Transaction:** Traded **Scroll of Scorching Ray** + 50gp (Paid by Elara) for **Scroll of Suggestion**.
- **Lore:** Varrick explained that *Silence* is a hard counter to Bards, preventing verbal spell components.
- **Lore:** Varrick explained that Paladins cannot learn spells from scrolls; *Mirror Image* is arcane and incompatible with Kriv.
- **Transaction:** Traded **Scroll of Silence** for 100gp store credit.
- **Transaction:** Used 100gp credit + 50gp (Kriv) to buy **Scroll of Flaming Sphere** for Felix.
- **Negotiation:** Kriv offered the Adaptive Ooze recipe to Varrick with a "no resale" condition. Varrick offered 200gp.
- **Haggling:** Kriv countered for 250gp + Intel. Varrick accepted.
- **Transaction:** Sold Adaptive Ooze Recipe for 250gp. Received intel on Vane Family (Corrupted Paladins, Sealed Manor).
- **Haggling:** Kriv offered Felix's old spellbook + 100gp for the **Cloak of Protection** (Value 350gp).
- **Counter-Offer:** Varrick valued the book at 50gp and countered with **200gp + Book** (Total 250gp value, 100gp discount).
- **Transaction:** Accepted Varrick's deal. Paid 200gp (Split) + Old Spellbook for **Cloak of Protection**.
- **Loot Distribution:** Kriv gave the **Cloak of Protection** to Felix.
- **Travel:** Party moved to the Royal Academy.
- **Interaction:** Met with Alchemist Vanya in Lab 4.
- **Reward:** Received **3x Alchemical Firebombs** derived from the Thermal Jelly research.
- **Intel:** The Virulent Jelly sample is unstable and dangerous.
- **Action:** Kriv used his Dark-Steel Greatsword to absorb the unstable Virulent Jelly sample.
- **Result:** The sample is neutralized. Kriv's sword is temporarily charged with poison.
- **Experiment:** Elara used her Dagger of Venom to re-poison the neutral residue, creating a sealed **Iron Cask of Virulent Jelly**.
- **Interaction:** Vanya agreed to test the Potion of Healing and Scroll of Ray of Sickness under Kriv's supervision.
- **Experiment:** Vanya added a Potion of Healing to the neutral ooze.
- **Result:** Critical Failure. The ooze mutated into a rapidly expanding **Regenerating Ooze**.
- **Crisis Resolution:** Elara dumped the Virulent Jelly on the main mass, neutralizing it.
- **Action:** Kriv activated the emergency containment, trapping a live sample of the Regenerating Ooze.
- **Status:** Lab secured. Regenerating Ooze sample acquired (Stationary/Contained).
- **Departure:** Kriv warned Vanya about the dangers of Necromancy experiments. Party left the Academy.
- **Decision:** Kriv decided to postpone the Vane Manor investigation to avoid fighting spirits at night.
- **Travel:** Party headed to the Grand Coliseum.
- **Registration:** Kriv signed the party (Kriv, Elara, Felix) up for **The Beast Hunt** vs a Wyvern. Scraps remains in the stables.
- **Shopping:** Party is browsing the arena vendor stalls.
- **Transaction:** Bought 3x Antitoxin (150gp). Cost split: Kriv 60gp, Felix 60gp, Elara 30gp.
- **Movement:** Party moved to the Arena Waiting Room.
- **Preparation:** Party consumed Antitoxins (Advantage vs Poison).
- **Buffs:** Kriv cast *Bless* (Party). Felix cast *Mage Armor* and *False Life* (+7 Temp HP).
- **Combat Start:** The Beast Hunt vs Wyvern.
- **Initiative:** Kriv (18), Elara (16), Wyvern (14), Felix (10).
